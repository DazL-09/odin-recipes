Roasted Garlic Parmesan Fingerling

Description

This best ever meatloaf recipe lives up to its name and is my favorite I have found. Try it
and you will see.

  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 slices bread, crumbled
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded carrot
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and ground black pepper in a large bowl. Add crumbled
    bread and stir until dissolved. Mix ground beef, onion, Cheddar cheese, and
    carrot into bread mixture; transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard in a small bowl; spread over the
    meat mixture.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 60 to 75 minutes.
    An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160
    degrees F (70 degrees C).
